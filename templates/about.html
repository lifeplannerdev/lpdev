Who We Are
Life Planner Universal

Life Planner — Established in 2012, Life Planner has grown from a modest start-up to become a trusted and well-respected educational facilitator among study abroad consultants in Kottayam. Recognised with ISO certification, we have earned a reputation for providing trustworthy and personalised services.

At Life Planner, we provide individualized counseling based on each student's distinct talents, interests, and study skills, ensuring a smooth transition through the admission and visa processes. Our experienced team provides admission support for medical, engineering, paramedical, nursing, IT, technical, management, and various other academic streams across globally renowned universities and institutions.

To ensure students achieve excellent IELTS or German language scores, we provide expert training at our dedicated academy. International students also benefit from our assistance in finding employment after graduation, supported by our alumni network and Immigration Consultant Office.

Life Planner operates from its headquarters in Kottayam and its corporate office in Cochin. We ensure our students settle comfortably upon arrival, offering airport pickup and accommodation assistance through close coordination with university officials and our alumni network.

Foreign Languages Academy Gurukulam (F.L.A.G.)

The language wing of Life Planner Studies & Opportunities Pvt. Ltd., established in 2018 as a premier language institute in Kerala. With over six years of expertise, F.L.A.G. has become a leader in German language training — the only authorised German Skill Development Centre in Kerala, collaborating with German companies to deliver industry-specific training.

Chairman's Message
Sujin C Cherian
Chairman & Founder — Life Planner
Overseas education can seem overwhelming for students and their parents, not because of its complexity, but because it offers an abundance of opportunities and choices. Selecting the right program, university, and destination is often a challenging decision for aspiring students, with the risk of being misled.
Every student has their own vision, passion, skills, and talents. Education should serve as a bridge to enhance this vision and bring those dreams to life. At Life Planner, we understand this better than anyone. We are well-connected with a network of world-class universities, colleges, and B-schools across the globe, enabling us to offer personalised solutions for each student.
Life Planner was founded with a true vision — to transform the way students experience overseas education. From the moment you join hands with us, your journey is carefully planned, step by step, so that you can focus on achieving your goals without any uncertainty. With Life Planner, you are not just choosing a consultancy; you are choosing a partner who is dedicated to helping you turn your dreams into reality.
