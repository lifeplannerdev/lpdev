Life Planner Established in 2012, Life Planner has grown from a modest start-up to become a trusted and well-respected educational facilitator among study abroad consultants in Kottayam. Recognised with ISO certification, we have earned a reputation for providing trustworthy and personalised services.

At Life Planner, we provide individualized counseling based on each student's distinct talents, interests, and study skills, ensuring a smooth transition through the admission and visa processes. Our experienced team provides admission support for medical, engineering, paramedical, nursing, IT, technical, management, and various other academic streams across globally renowned universities and institutions. Students can explore undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate programs in almost all countries.

To ensure students achieve excellent IELTS or German language scores, we provide expert training at our dedicated academy. International students also benefit from our assistance in finding employment after graduation, supported by our alumni network and Immigration Consultant Office. Our commitment does not end once students reach their destination; we continue to provide guidance and support, reinforcing our standing as one of the leading overseas education consultants in Kottayam.

Life Planner Studies and Opportunities Pvt Ltd operates from its headquarters in Kottayam and its corporate office in Cochin. We ensure our students settle comfortably upon arrival, offering airport pickup and accommodation assistance. This is achieved through close coordination with university or college officials and our former students who are now successfully settled abroad. The wholehearted participation of our alumni is a testament to the exceptional service they received during their journey with us. This enduring trust has positioned Life Planner as the top study abroad consultant in Kottayam, Kerala.

Chairman’s Message

Overseas education can seem overwhelming for students and their parents, not because of its complexity, but because it offers an abundance of opportunities and choices. Selecting the right program, university, and destination is often a challenging decision for aspiring students, with the risk of being misled.

Think about it—we choose clothes based on our size and preferred colour, food according to our taste, and even our perspective on a subject is shaped by personal experiences and interests. In short, every decision we make is uniquely ours, tailored to fit who we are. So why, when it comes to education, do so many feel pressured to follow trends? Why do we rush to join the crowd, pursuing programs, universities, or countries that may not truly align with our dreams and abilities?

Every student has their own vision, passion, skills, and talents. Education should serve as a bridge to enhance this vision and bring those dreams to life. At Life Planner, we understand this better than anyone. We are well-connected with a network of world-class universities, colleges, and B-schools across the globe, enabling us to offer personalised solutions for each student.

Our strategy is simple yet effective: we provide tailor-made guidance based on your preferences, strengths, and aspirations. This process is carried out through our highly trained and experienced counsellors, who not only guide you towards the right academic options but also suggest clear and legal pathways for immigration.

We are committed to ensuring that every student receives an uncompromised quality of education and unbiased counselling, so they can make well-informed decisions. To maintain this promise, I, as the Chairman of Life Planner, personally visit many of the universities and institutions we recommend. I do this to verify their facilities, educational standards, and post-study opportunities, ensuring they meet the expectations we set for our students.

Life Planner was founded with a true vision—to transform the way students experience overseas education. From the moment you join hands with us, your journey is carefully planned, step by step, so that you can focus on achieving your goals without any uncertainty.

With Life Planner, you are not just choosing a consultancy; you are choosing a partner who is dedicated to helping you turn your dreams into reality.
