Ausbildung — Dual Vocational Training

Germany's renowned dual vocational training system combines real workplace experience with classroom learning. Earn a monthly stipend while training at a company or hospital, attend vocational school, and graduate with a recognised German qualification — no tuition fees required.

Dual Vocational Training · 1–3 Years · Germany

Program Highlights

The Ausbildung is Germany's renowned dual vocational training system — you split your time between a vocational school (Berufsschule) and a real employer such as a company or hospital. This means you gain hands-on industry experience from day one while receiving a monthly training stipend. Over 100 recognised training fields are available, spanning healthcare, IT, engineering, hospitality, and more. Upon completion you receive an official German vocational qualification with full EU work rights.
