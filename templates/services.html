OUR SERVICE

Seminars & Webinars

  • Informative seminars and webinars provide detailed guidance on studying abroad.
  • Cover essential aspects, including university programs, admission requirements, and more.
  • Interactive online sessions are available for those unable to attend in person.
  • Live Q&A sessions address specific queries from students.
  • Post-seminar, each student receives:
    • One-on-one counselling to discuss academic and career aspirations.
    • Support from an admission executive throughout the application process.

Academic & Career Counselling

  • Comprehensive evaluation of the student’s academic background and work experience.
  • Language proficiency assessment, particularly in German.
  • Match students with suitable academic programs and career opportunities.
  • Evaluate the student’s financial readiness for studying and living abroad.

Profile Assessment

  • Personalised counselling based on the profile assessment.
  • Identify the fields that align best with the student’s interests and goals.
  • Recommend skill development programs, such as:
    • Improving German language proficiency.
    • Preparing for exams like IELTS.
  • Provide insights into the job market and conduct:
  • Mock interviews to boost confidence.
  • Industry-specific guidance for future career success.

Admission & Enrollment

  • Guide students through the admission and enrollment process.
  • Assist in preparing application materials, including:
    • Resumes.
    • Personal statements.
    • Cover letters tailored to university requirements.
  • Provide details on program fees, duration, curriculum, and focus.
  • Support students with language proficiency requirements in German and English.

Financial Aid Guidance

  • Help students understand the financial requirements for studying abroad.
  • Provide guidance on visa-related financial documentation, such as:
    • Bank statements.
    • Financial sponsorship letters.
  • Explore available financial aid options, including scholarships and student loans.

Visa Processing Assistance

  • Full guidance on meeting visa requirements and selecting the correct visa type.
  • Assist in gathering and preparing the required visa documentation.
  • Ensure all documents meet embassy standards.
  • Prepare students for their visa interview to maximise success.

Pre-Departure Briefing

  • Provide a comprehensive pre-departure session covering:
  • Travel details, including flight bookings.
  • Accommodation arrangements at the study destination.
  • Country-specific guidelines for a smooth transition.
  • Ensure students feel prepared for their move abroad and understand next steps.

Post-Landing Assistance

  • Support students upon arrival with:
  • Immigration procedures.
  • Guidance on transport options.
  • Assist students in settling into their new environment.
  • Provide ongoing support to help students adjust confidently to their new life abroad.
