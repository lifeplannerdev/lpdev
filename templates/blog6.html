Study in Germany from Kerala – Eligibility, Process & Immigration

Germany has emerged as a top destination for higher education among students from Kerala. Known for its world-class universities, practical-oriented curriculum, and strong job market, Germany offers excellent academic and professional opportunities.

This comprehensive guide explains the eligibility criteria, step-by-step application process, and immigration opportunities for students from Kerala aspiring to study in Germany in 2025.

Why Study in Germany from Kerala?

Germany is not just about engineering and automobiles. It's a hub for academic excellence, offering programs in English and German across disciplines like science, technology, business, arts, and healthcare.

High-Quality Education

Germany is home to some of the best public universities globally that offer tuition-free or low-cost education with top research facilities.

English-Taught Programs

A vast number of Master's programs are available in English, especially in engineering, business, and IT.

Post-Study Work

After graduation, students are eligible for an 18-month job-seeking visa.

Path to PR

Germany offers clear immigration policies that help international students settle permanently.

Eligibility to Study in Germany from Kerala

Academic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Programs: Completion of 12th grade with minimum 70% marks. Some programs may require preparatory courses.
  • Master's Programs: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Four-year degrees preferred, though three-year degrees with excellent scores may be accepted.

Language Proficiency

  • English-Taught Programs: TOEFL or other accepted English proficiency tests
  • German-Taught Programs: B1/B2 level proficiency (Goethe Institute or TestDaF)

APS Certification (New Requirement)

As of 2022, Indian students need an Academic Evaluation Centre (APS) certificate from the German Embassy to validate academic documents.

Other Documents

Statement of Purpose (SOP), Letters of Recommendation (LORs), Resume/CV, passport, and academic transcripts.

How to Apply to Study in Germany from Kerala

1. Shortlist Universities and Programs

Identify courses and universities that suit your academic background and career goals. Use official platforms like DAAD or get help from a reliable study abroad consultancy like Life Planner.

2. Check Eligibility and Admission Requirements

Each university may have its own criteria for GPA, entrance tests, and language proficiency.

3. Apply for APS Certification

Apply through the APS India website. Submit academic documents, passport, and pay the processing fee. APS verification is now mandatory for all Indian students.

4. Apply to Universities

Applications can be made via Uni-Assist or directly through university websites. Deadlines:

  • Winter Intake (starts Sept/Oct): Apply April-July
  • Summer Intake (starts March/April): Apply November-January

5. Receive Admission Offer

You will receive either a conditional or confirmed offer letter.

6. Prepare Supporting Documents

Get health insurance, proof of accommodation, passport, photos, SOP, and financial proof ready for visa purposes.

7. Schedule Visa Appointment

Book your student visa appointment at the German Consulate or VFS center (Kochi for Kerala students). Required documents:

  • University admission letter
  • APS certificate
  • Language certificate
  • Health insurance
  • Proof of accommodation

8. Travel and Enroll

After visa approval, travel to Germany and complete university enrollment before starting classes.

Post-Study Immigration After Studying in Germany

Germany offers excellent immigration policies for international students:

18-Month Job Search Visa

International graduates can apply for an 18-month job search visa to work in any field while seeking employment in their qualification area.

EU Blue Card

Once you find a relevant job, apply for the EU Blue Card (work permit for highly qualified non-EU citizens).

Permanent Residency

After working for 33 months (or 21 months with B1-level German), you can apply for PR. Requirements include:

  • Valid employment contract
  • German language proficiency (minimum B1)
  • Health insurance and pension contributions
  • Clean criminal record

Family Sponsorship

Sponsor your spouse and children once you have stable income and accommodation. Spouses can work full-time.

FAQs on Studying in Germany from Kerala

Is an English test mandatory for studying in Germany?

It depends on the university and program. While many universities accept various English proficiency certificates, check specific requirements on university websites.

Can I study in Germany without knowing German?

Yes, many programs are available in English. However, learning German is beneficial for daily life and job opportunities.

What is the APS Certificate and how long does it take?

APS is mandatory academic verification for Indian students. Processing may take 3-4 weeks, so apply early.

Can I get PR after studying in Germany?

Yes, by working for 2–4 years and meeting other criteria like language proficiency and employment status.
